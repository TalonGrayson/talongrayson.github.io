To get started using your Tagonist bases, start by downloading NFC Tools for your device.

Other apps will also work, this is just the one I prefer. It's free, which is good.

I am in no way affiliated with the developers of this app.

Once you have it installed, you can use it to write any data you like.

With your data written to the base, just close the app and scan the base with your phone.

This is just a quick-start page to get you up and running. What you do with your bases is up to you!
